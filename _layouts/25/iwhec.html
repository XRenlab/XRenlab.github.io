International Workshop on Human-Engaged Computing 2025

人机共协计算国际研讨会暨人机共协计算新书发布会

新加坡管理大学，新加坡
2025年11月22日 13:00~17:40

免费注册

人机共协计算国际研讨会（International Workshop on Human-Engaged Computing， IWHEC 2024）立足于重新思考人与计算机之间的关系。通过邀请来自人机交互、脑科学、人文哲社、设计、计算机科学和人工智能等多个领域的专家分享他们的见解，我们期待于此搭建一个集智平台，促进全球思想交流。本届研讨会将采用混合形式进行，您可以选择亲临南方科技大学参与或通过直播观看。

在过去几年中，您可能亲身经历过ICT技术对人类的支持和潜在负面影响，以及人工智能取得的里程碑式发展，而这些现象对我们来说意味着什么？基于我们所拥有的领域知识，通过对人与技术的关系展开充分讨论，我们希望对未来研究和技术发展之路得出一些见解、共识和实践方向。

    曾任中国科学院软件研究所总工程师，现任人机交互技术与智能信息处理实验室首席研究员, 博士生导师。毕业于中国科学技术大学，1982-1985年在马里兰大学计算机系做访问学者。曾任国家863计划自动化领域专家组成员和专家委员会成员，曾任国家973计划信息领域咨询专家。ACM SIGCHI中国分会名誉主席，中国计算机学会人机交互专业委员会名譽主任。 戴国忠研究员主持和参加了多项国家自然科学基金项目、国家高技术研究发展计划项目、国家重点基础研究发展规划项目。主要研究领域为计算机图形学、人机交互、软件工程。在国内外有影响的会议和刊物上发表论文百余篇，专著2部。四次获得国家科技进步奖，多次获中科院和部委科技进步奖。

    Dai Guozhong is a distinguished researcher at the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS). He previously served as the Chief Engineer of the Institute of Software at CAS and is currently the Chief Researcher of the Human-Computer Interaction Technology and Intelligent Information Processing Laboratory, as well as a doctoral supervisor. Dai graduated from the University of Science and Technology of China and was a visiting scholar in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland from 1982 to 1985. He has held significant roles, including serving as a member of the expert group and expert committee for the Automation Field of the National 863 Program, and as a consulting expert for the Information Field of the National 973 Program. Additionally, he is the Honorary Chair of ACM SIGCHI China and the Honorary Director of the Human-Computer Interaction Special Committee of the China Computer Federation.

    Throughout his career, Researcher Guozhong Dai has led and participated in numerous projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the National High-Tech Research and Development Program, and the National Basic Research Program. His primary research interests include computer graphics, human-computer interaction, and software engineering. He has published over 100 papers in influential domestic and international conferences and journals, and has authored two monographs. Dai has been recognized with the National Science and Technology Progress Award four times and has received multiple awards for scientific and technological progress from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and other governmental ministries and commissions.
