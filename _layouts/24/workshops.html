Workshop Schedule (Nov 22 08:30 - 12:00)

Chinese CHI工作坊集中于人机交互、工业设计、用户体验和用户界面设计领域的任何相关领域，尤其是与今年会议主题有关的具有挑战性的新兴领域，涉及基础研究、应用研究、HCI实践、HCI教育、新方法、新兴应用领域或设计创新。

Chinese CHI workshops focus on any relevant fields in human-computer interaction, industrial design, user experience, and user interface design, especially in challenging emerging areas related to this year's conference theme, encompassing foundational research, applied research, HCI practices, HCI education, new methodologies, emerging application areas, or design innovations.

注（11月15日更新）：此付费已包含在Chinese CHI 2024会议全程票或22日单日票中。本注册仅面向无Chinese CHI 2024会议全程票注册的参会者，可注册“工作坊1：跨平台交互创新的新工具”、“工作坊2：生物HCI的可能性：探索生物HCI创新的未来场景”和“工作坊5：城市声境：城市声音日志的交互数据可视化”，其他工作坊须凭会议全程票或单日票参加。

This fee has been included in the Full Conference Ticket for the Chinese CHI 2024 conference or the single-day ticket for the 22nd. This registration is only for participants who do NOT have a full ticket for the Chinese CHI 2024 conference. You can register for “Workshop 1: A New Tool for Cross-Platform Interaction Innovation,” “Workshop 2: Towards New Possibilities of Bio-HCI: Exploring Future Scenarios of Bio-HCI Innovation,” and “Workshop 5: Exploring Urban Soundscapes: Interactive Data Visualization with ‘Urban Sound Diary’.” Other workshops require either a full conference ticket or a single-day ticket for participation.
